Overview

Purpose

Ever resumed to your hostel after a hard day's school work, only to find out that you can't cook because you're out of food supplies?, or you need to get a particular clothing you saw on campus, but don't know the right plug?, or the prices from online stores like jumia seem a little to high for you (an average college students),or are you a student that needs more customers for your side hustle, but aren't able to reach out to many?,...Well you're in luck, with our website built with campus and students in mind, you won't have to worry about these problems, as we are happy to connect to you to vendors who are students like yourself, that offers just the right services for you on campus. Unlike online stores like jumia or Amazon, we connect you directly to the vendor, providing you with a one way exchange of goods and services, giving you fast delivery, pocket friendly and customer centered experience,and for students who have side hustles, with our website, you have the opportunity to easily connect to students/customers directly, attending to their needs. With our website we'll make sure you never have to worry about getting that fast and valuable service you've always deserved.

Audience

This site is exclusively for campus students around the world. It is student centered, as both the customers and the vendorsare most likely students. Students who have side hustles as to maintain their living on campus have the opportunity to increase their customer base by getting instant connection to customers,delivering quality products anad services to fellow students Students who serve as customers get fast, quick and easy access to vendors and can get acccess to their services prompt. In esssence this site is for those who would like to ease their stress on campus
